Our Syllabus


Business Studies

1) Business Activity:
- Understand needs, wants and scarcity
- Understand the purpose of business activity
- Objectives of non-profit making activity,private enterprise and public enterprise

2) Motivating Workers:
- Why would people work?
- Benefits of well-motivated workers
- Maslow’s hierarchy
- Taylor’s theory of motivation
- Herzberg’s theory of motivation
- Financial incentives
- Non-financial(Fringe benefits)

3) Marketing, competition and the customer:
- Important P’s of marketing
- Marketing department in an organization
- Functions of marketing
- Marketing helps in-?
- Why are markets competitive?
- Defenition and types of markets
- Benefits of market segmentation and which one can used for a business


Computer Science


1) Types & Components of Computer Systems:
- Hardware and Software
- Types of Computer Emerging Technologies

2) Excel:
- Creating data models
- Cells
- Basic formulas
- Data organisation
- Graphs and Charts

3) Safety & Security:
- Safety issues
- Data Protection
- Personal Data

4) Psuedocode:
- Different Loops
- Flow charts
- Structure diagrams
- Purpose of an algorithm
- Minimum and maximum values
- Average values
- Totalling
- Counting
- Validation and verification

5) Databases:
- What are fields and records ?
- Types of validation
- SQL statements

6) Boolean logic:
- NOT,AND,OR,NOR,XOR,NAND gates
- Drawing gates
- Logic expression
- Truth tables

Enterprise

1) Setting up a new enterprise
- The different stakeholders
- The different stages of the enterprising process
- Key points to consider at each stage
- Characteristics of different types of business organisations

2) Skills and behaviour of enterprising people
- Skills of enterprising people
- How enterprising people have used their skills to be successful

3) Market research
- Methods of identifying potential customers
- Effectiveness of these methods for different enterprises
